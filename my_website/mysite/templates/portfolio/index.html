Think Eureka

Mini ERP

Eureka is ThikLABS science product. This product was hands on science products for the schools students ranging from 4th to 9th class and each class having at least 8 activities. The schools were allowed to customize the activities they want. When the orders flow in the orders are added to the system against the schools. The production team is intimated of the orders so they produce the activites accordingly. After the order is produced, the packing team packages it accordingly and the dispatch team dispatches the products. All these details are added to the system which allow the management to check the flow and keep a tab of the order. This also allows to have prediction for the next year.

  • Date: Dec 2014
  • Organization: ThinkLABS Pvt Ltd
  • Software Used: Python, Django, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML.
